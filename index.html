Dal Tadka

Easy
25 min 320 kcal
Ingredients
Lentils 200g Tomato 2 Onion 1 Garlic 4 cloves Cumin 1 tsp Turmeric ½ tsp
Instructions
1

Wash and soak lentils for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.

2

Heat oil in a pot. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.

3

Add chopped onion, garlic and sauté until golden brown.

4

Add tomatoes, turmeric, and cook till soft. Add lentils + 2 cups water.

5

Pressure cook for 3 whistles or simmer for 20 min until soft. Serve hot.
